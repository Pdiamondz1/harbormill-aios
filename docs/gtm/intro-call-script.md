# Intro Call Script + Qualification (Free 30 min)

Goal of the call: **qualify, make the pain a number, and book the paid Loop Audit before you hang up.**
Not to pitch a build. Not to give free consulting away. Guide, don't sell.

---

## The 30-minute structure

### 1. Their world — 10 min (listen, don't pitch)
Open: *"Before I say anything about what I do — tell me about the business. What's eating your week
right now? Where does time or money leak?"*

Listen for the three high-ROI loops:
- **AR / invoicing** — "we're slow to invoice / chasing payments / money stuck in overdue invoices."
- **Leads** — "leads come in by email/call and get re-keyed / dropped / followed up late."
- **Reporting** — "I build the same report by hand every week / I'm flying blind on numbers."

Probe: *"How often does that happen? Roughly how long each time? What's it costing you?"*

### 2. Make the prize a number — 5 min
Reflect their pain back as dollars, live:
- AR: *"So ~$40k in overdue invoices, paying ~30 days slow. Even recovering a third faster is ~$13k
  back in your account, and it never stops once it's automated."*
- Leads: *"If you're dropping ~5 leads a month at a $2k value and 20% close — that's ~$24k/yr walking out."*

The number is what makes them want the audit. Don't skip it.

### 3. Teach the method — 5 min (positions you as the guide)
*"Here's how I decide what's actually worth automating — I call it the Four-Condition Loop Test. A
task is worth it only if: it **repeats**, a **clear rule** says when it's done, a **wrong run is cheap**,
and the **data + tools already exist**. AR follow-up nails all four. That's why I'd look there first —
but the audit is how we confirm it against everything else on your plate."*

### 4. Offer the Loop Audit — 5 min
*"The next step is a Loop Audit. I map all your repeating work, score each task on those four
conditions, and hand you a ranked plan with the single highest-ROI thing to build first — the
reasoning included, a plan you keep whether or not we build it together. It's **$500–$2,500 fixed
scope, and I credit the whole fee toward the build if you decide to go ahead.** Want to grab a time
this week?"*

### 5. Book it before you hang up — 5 min
Put the audit discovery on the calendar live. Set the deliverable date (report within 48h).

---

## Qualification checklist (BANT-lite — disqualify fast, protect your hours)

| Signal | Green (proceed) | Red (refer + exit gracefully) |
|---|---|---|
| **Repeating pain** | Clear, frequent, costly loop | No real recurring busywork |
| **Budget** | Can fund a $2.5–10k project | "No budget for anything" |
| **Authority** | Owner / decision-maker | "I'd have to ask someone" (book that someone) |
| **Urgency** | Pain is felt now | "Maybe next year" |

**Graceful exit (no fit):** *"Honestly I don't think a paid build makes sense for you right now —
I'd rather tell you that than take your money. Who's one other owner you think I should talk to?"*
(Even a no harvests a referral.)

---

## Compression option (for warm, eager prospects)
If they're clearly ready, **run the audit discovery right there** (extend the call or book it for the
next day) and deliver the Opportunity Report within 48h. Shortens the cycle inside the 30-day clock.
