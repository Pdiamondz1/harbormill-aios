# Outreach + Referral Templates

Warm, non-salesy, double-duty (book intros AND harvest referrals). Personalize the first line every
time — the warmth is the whole point. Send ~10–15/day, A-tier first.

---

## 1. Warm opener (primary)

> Hey [name] — I've gone all-in on building practical AI automation for small businesses (the
> boring, repetitive stuff that quietly eats an owner's week). I'm doing a handful of **free 30-min
> sessions** this month where I map someone's repeating work and point at the **one automation
> worth building first** — no pitch, you keep the plan either way.
>
> Two quick asks: (1) would that be useful for you or your team? And (2) who's one business owner
> you'd say I should be talking to? Either way — good to reconnect.

*Why it works:* gives value (free session), low-commitment, and the referral ask means even a "not
me" reply still grows the funnel.

---

## 2. Referral-first (for B-tier connectors who aren't buyers)

> Hey [name] — quick one. I'm building AI automation for small businesses and looking to talk to a
> few owners drowning in repetitive busywork (manual invoicing/AR, lead entry, weekly reports).
> You know everyone — is there **one or two people** you'd point me to? Happy to make it worth your
> while, and I'll keep it low-key and useful for them (free 30-min, no hard sell).

---

## 3. Follow-up (no reply after 3–4 days)

> Hey [name] — floating this back up in case it got buried. Still happy to do a free 30-min on the
> automation worth building first for [their business]. If now's not the time, totally fine — even
> just one name of an owner I should talk to would help a ton.

*One follow-up only. Then mark dormant and move on.*

---

## 4. Warm-intro handoff (when a referrer connects you)

> Thanks for the intro, [referrer]! [Name] — great to meet you. [Referrer] mentioned you [context].
> I help small businesses kill the repetitive busywork with AI — the quickest start is a free 30-min
> where I map your repeating work and flag the one thing worth automating first. Here's my calendar:
> [Calendly link]. Grab whatever works.

---

## 5. LinkedIn post angle (credibility, 2–3/wk — not a closer, a warmer)

Rotate these themes; end each with a soft "DM me / book a free 30-min":
- A real automation you built and the hours/dollars it saved (concrete numbers).
- The **Four-Condition Loop Test** explained on one example (teaches + positions you as the guide).
- "Most shops sell a black box. I'd rather teach you to fish" — the education-first story.
- A myth-bust: "AI won't replace your staff — it kills the copy-paste so they do the human work."
