# Sweven Membership Audit

The first move of the Sweven layer: turn the membership of the Sweven workspace into (1) the
**beachhead agency sub-type** to concentrate on and (2) a **warm pilot shortlist** that flows
straight into the [Warm-50 tracker](warm-50-tracker.md). Sweven *is* a ready-made Warm-50+ — this
audit is how we read it. Pairs with [beachhead-decision.md](beachhead-decision.md) (the output) and
the [Sweven channel playbook](sweven-channel.md) (the motion).

> Goal: categorize ≥ 60% of members in one pass, in < 5 min each. Copy into a Sheet/Airtable for
> live tracking; this markdown is the schema + the survey.

---

## The micro-survey (ask in person or async)

Keep it to four questions — anything longer kills response rate at a coworking space:

1. **What does your business actually do?** (one line)
2. **What's the most repetitive thing that eats your week** — the boring stuff you'd hand off first?
3. **What tools does that run on today?** (CRM, spreadsheet, inbox, accounting, etc.)
4. **If I mapped your repeating work for free and pointed at the one thing worth automating first —
   useful?** (Y/N — this is the soft open for the [intro call](intro-call-script.md).)

## Categorization schema

| Member | Business (one line) | `agency_sub_type` | `revenue_band` | Biggest repeating pain | Tools | `open_to_pilot` | → Warm-50 tier |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| _e.g._ Alex R. | Paid-social agency, 4 staff | marketing/SMMA | $250k–$1M | Manual lead follow-up + weekly client reports | HubSpot, Sheets | Y | A |
| | | | | | | | |

**`agency_sub_type` values:** `marketing/SMMA` · `creative/design` · `dev/technical` · `recruiting` ·
`PR` · `other-agency` · `non-agency-service` (e.g. the restaurant) · `solo-founder` (Phase-2 audience).

**`revenue_band`:** `<$250k` · `$250k–$1M` · `$1M+`.

**Pain shorthand** (nudge toward the high-ROI loops the [intro script](intro-call-script.md) listens for):
`AR` (slow invoicing / chasing payment) · `leads` (lead intake / follow-up dropped) ·
`reporting` (hand-built reports) · `onboarding` (client/project onboarding) · `other`.

## What happens next

- The dominant agency sub-type + the pilot shortlist are written up in
  [beachhead-decision.md](beachhead-decision.md).
- Every `open_to_pilot = Y` member is added to [warm-50-tracker.md](warm-50-tracker.md) as an
  **A-tier** contact (they're warm *and* a buyer) — the Sweven channel's whole advantage is that the
  top of the funnel is pre-warmed.
- Non-agency service businesses (the partner's restaurant, etc.) are routed to the **dogfood /
  flagship case-study** track, not the paid beachhead.
- Solo founders are tagged for the **Phase-2 builder/community** audience — parked, not pitched now.
