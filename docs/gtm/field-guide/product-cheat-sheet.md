## The 1-breath thesis
An **AI operating deck you own** — it watches your numbers, writes your weekly brief, and runs the
repeating work, while teaching you to drive the AI yourself. The moat is the **context layer** that
compounds: the longer you run it, the more it knows your business.

## The ladder, in one line
**AI Setup Session** ($250 fixed, 90 min — the low-friction door) → **Loop Audit** ($500–$2,500
fixed, *whole fee credited* toward the build) → **Focused Project** (**$5,000 fixed** — audit fee
credited, *50% deposit / 50% on delivery*) → **Retainer** (Operate **$3,000/mo** · Operate + Build
**$5,000/mo** · Embedded **$8–10k/mo**).

**Every price is fixed. We do not quote hourly** — we sell the elimination of billable hours, so
an hourly rate prices us against our own product. ($100/hr exists as an unpublished one-off
fallback; never open with it.)

## The 4 Loop conditions (the Four-Condition Loop Test)
A task is a Loop candidate *only if it passes all four*:
1. **It repeats** — predictable cadence (daily / weekly / per-event). Frequency × time = the prize.
2. **A rule decides "done"** — an *objective* check, not human taste. *(hard blocker)*
3. **You can afford a wrong run** — advisory or easily undone, can't blow up the business.
4. **The AI has the data + tools** — inputs reachable, actions exist as real tools. *(hard blocker)*

> 2 & 4 are hard blockers; 1 & 3 scored strong/partial/weak. Among passers, rank by
> **value-per-effort** and build the highest first. Our live proof point: **KPI-watch**, the Loop we
> run on our own systems — "the same discipline we run on us."

## The 6 surfaces a prospect cares about
- **Overview** — your business in one glance: live numbers + what we've delivered vs. the fee.
- **Briefings** — a weekly plain-English brief on what moved and what to watch.
- **Aria** — ask anything about your numbers; it answers from your real data, not guesses.
- **Loops** — where your automations live; risky actions wait here for a human "go."
- **Value** — exactly what this engagement produced in dollars, vs. promise and fee.
- **Connectors** — wire your existing tools straight in, so your numbers update themselves.
